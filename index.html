About Me

M.S. Applied Data Science at The University of Chicago

Hello!
My name is Minh. I am originally from Vietnam. I got my Bachelor's in Business Analytics at University of South Florida. During my undergraduate studies, I did three internships in business intelligence, sales operations and data analytics, which strongly helped me with my data analytics, dashboaring, and storytelling skills when I got to work on huge datasets as well as build dashboards using SQL, Python, and Tableau to generate valuable insights and present to the business stakeholders, helping them improve their business outcomes.

Those internships were pivotal in shaping my understanding of the role of data across various industries. This realization propelled me towards pursuing a Master's degree in Data Science at the University of Chicago. Here, I delved deeper into data science and machine learning, exploring various analytical methodologies to tackle complex problems in interdisciplinary areas.

As a self-motivated individual, I am always eager to learn new things. My passion is to harness the power of data-driven insights for informed decision-making, optimizing processes, and uncovering new opportunities for businesses and organizations. I am excited about the journey ahead and the possibilities that lie in the intersection of data, technology, and business.
